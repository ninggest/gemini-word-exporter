# Privacy Policy for Gemini Word Exporter

**Last updated: February 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Gemini Word Exporter ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your information.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.**

*   **Local Processing**: All operations, including extracting chat content and generating Word documents, are performed locally within your browser on your device.
*   **No Server Transmission**: The data extracted from Google Gemini is processed in your browser's memory and is never sent to our servers or any third-party servers.
*   **User Settings**: Your preferences (such as custom motto, address, and logo) are stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API and are not synced to any external server.

## 2. Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions for specific functional purposes:

*   **activeTab**: To access the content of the current Gemini chat page only when you click the extension icon.
*   **scripting**: To parse the chat content for document generation.
*   **storage**: To save your formatting preferences locally.
*   **downloads**: To save the generated .docx file to your computer.
*   **offscreen**: To handle complex document generation tasks in the background without freezing your browser.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with `https://gemini.google.com` solely to read the content you wish to export. It does not interact with any other websites or services.

## 4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
